Our Mission

The I DID application is all about helping you reach your goals. Whatever that goal may be. These are some testimonials from others who have used our applicataion. Our aim is to foster a sense of achievement in you. Think of good things you have done today towards your goal. By reflecting on the positives in our day this motivates us to continue striving towards our goals. As you reflect on all the tasks you managed to accomplish for the day you realise how much you actually accomplished. Too often we are so consumed in our lives to reflect on our achievements so they go unrecognised and we feel stagnated. This application is to show you the true value of your efforts. All achievements no matter how big or small deserve to be noted and celebrated. You can achieve your goal one task at a time.

Set Goal

Set any goal you wish to achieve.




Note Achievements

At the end of each day note all positive things which added towards that goal.

Reflect

Read over all your previous entries. Look at how much you have accomplished. You can do this!


Reach Goal

Cross the finish line and mark off your goal. Finally all the things you have done have added up to this point- where you have finally reached your goal.
